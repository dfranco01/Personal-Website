My Mission Statement

INTEGRITY: Even when no one is watching, still acting in alignment with my values at all times, in order to maintain the highest opinion of myself.
COURAGE: Despite my fears, I face them time and time again.

PERSEVERANCE: willingly retrying every tribulation I failed, knowing full well the hardships that awaited me each time. I will accomplish the mission, even if I run through hell multiple times.

PERSONAL RESPONSIBILITY: Understanding that my not having the things I wanted was all on me and deciding to act to change that.

PROFESSIONALISM: keeping myself well-groomed, well-dressed, and physically fit at all times. Speaking audibly and intelligently

ADAPTABILITY: adapting and succeeding in both military and civilian life, embracing change despite the mass uncertainty

TEAM PLAYER: Helping the people I care about achieve personal victories in their life

CAPABILITY: taking action to achieve my goals despite all my self-doubts and uncertainty

UNDERSTANDING THE OPERATIONAL ENVIRONMENT: reading the room, adjusting my behavior accordingly, and having set strategies depending on who I am interacting with. Being able to switch communication styles as needed.

QUALITY OVER QUANTITY: a few, tight-knit friends over a mediocre, vast network

HUMANS ARE MORE IMPORTANT THAN MACHINES: AI is not my friend and never will be. It is nothing but a servant who serves human interests. As a professional entering the tech space, it is my job and responsibility to ensure it remains that way.
